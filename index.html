About Brew Review

Uncle & niece, one cup at a time ☕

This is Kara & Joel's personal journal for exploring San Francisco's coffee and matcha scene. We rate every café across seven categories — ambiance, coffee selection, creativity, coffee art, coffee quality, service, and a bonus food score when it applies — each on a 1–10 scale. Your two scores get averaged into one honest number.

How rating works

Add a café, punch in both Kara's and Joel's numbers for each category, and we'll average your scores per category and then roll them into a single final rating. Hit Save and it lands in “Been There.” Everything is editable anytime — your data lives right in this browser.
